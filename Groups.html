College Students

State school campuses are at odds with the Christian faith.
Our ministry provides a way for college age Christians to pursue righteousness, faith, love and peace, along with others who call on the Lord out of a pure heart, so that they may set an example for the believers in speech, in life, in love, in faith and in purity.
With God giving the increase, we have watched our group, and our whole congregation, be strengthened in faith and in grow in numbers.
We see our young men lead singing and speak to the Body. We see our young women help with children's classes and reach out in evangelism.
A few things our college group has come to expect: Sunday potluck Tuesday coffee shop Bible study Wednesday night supper Thursday lunch Friday game night Saturday football games Weekend waterfall walks, Christmas shopping for the children's home, winter retreats.
Please pray for God's blessing, so that we may become a place that churches in South Carolina look forward to sending their college students.
If you or someone you know is considering Clemson, Tri-County Tech, or another school in our area, we would love to hear from you.
(Passages referenced above: 2 Tim. 2:22, 1 Tim 4:12, 1 Cor. 3:6, Acts 16:5)
